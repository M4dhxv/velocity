Can I cancel anytime? add
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
How does gig matching work? add
Our AI engine scans live gig postings in real time and matches them to your skills, location, and availability. The more you complete, the better your matches get.
When do I get paid? add
Payouts are processed within 24 hours of task confirmation. Most workers receive payment the same day via their connected wallet or bank account.
What is Velocity AI? add
Velocity AI is our intelligent assistant available on the Pro plan. It auto-applies to gigs, negotiates on your behalf, and helps you optimise your earning schedule.
